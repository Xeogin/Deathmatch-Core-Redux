Changes made to this server for fun:
    

  • Added a lot of event and say sounds. More will be added as thought of.
    • 

  • Adding the best bots I could find and tweaked them as best I could. Expect to be killed.
    • 

  • All body parts have different damage multipliers. Head is 5, chest + stomach is 3, arms are 2 and legs are 1.5.
    • 

  • Charging speed has been multiplied by 2. Super chargers max out at 150 rather than 200.
    • 

  • Due to the multipliers being in effect, damage of all hitscan guns has been slightly tweaked. Shotgun reg feels better.
    • 

  • Movement speed has been multipled by 1.25 and gravity reduced to 450.
    • 

  • Respawn time for all destructable props is now 2 minutes, even if they didn't respawn before.
    • 

  • Small props and the trap blades can be walked through when they're not moving, so there's less tripping and getting stuck.
    • 

  • Some ammo spawns have been swapped around. It's both for balance and to freshen things up.
    • 

  • You spawn with a crossbow and 15 armor. I recommend you get moving quickly and make good use of them.
    • 



Changes to patch issues with game itself:
    

  • Removed most sound effects that can get caught in loop. This has the side effect of reducing explosion damage a bit.
    • 

  • The idle "T" body animation that tends to appear while you're in the air is a lot less likely to appear.

  • The small gas tanks explosions now do reasonable damage rather than only 1 hp.

  • Weapons priorites have been altered so it's less likely your gun will autoswitch them you pick up ammo and the like.
