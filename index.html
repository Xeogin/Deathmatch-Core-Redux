Changes made to this server for fun:
    

  • Some item spawns have been swapped around. It's both for balance and to freshen things up.
    • 

  • Movement speed has been multipled by 1.25 and gravity reduced to 400.
    • 

  • Charging speed has been multiplied by 2. Super chargers max out at 150 rather than 200.
    • 

  • Respawn time for destructable props has been multiplied by 2.
    • 

  • The pistol damage has been reduced by 1, ar2 and shotgun pellets by 2, and magnum by 30.
    • 

  • The default ammo for a few weapons has been reduced, such as the RPG and slams to 1.
    • 

  • Small props and the trap blades can be walked through when they're not moving, so there's less tripping and getting stuck.
    • 

  • Added ability to play as more HL2 characters. The switcher doesn't seem to change your underlying model though...
    • 

  • You spawn with a crossbow and 15 armor.
    • 

  • Added lots of event and say sounds.
    •
