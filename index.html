Jan 2022 Update:

It took a while, but all the functionality I wanted to rebuild after the server was lost is restored and/or improved! The server is also starting to go in another direction, all feedback is welcome as the new goal is to increase the player base. :)

Game Modes:

  • Deathmatch: This is the standard mode, it also allows for Team Deathmatch and Competitive match mode

  • Bots: Face off against some of the most realistic bots in the game, due to engine limitations they’re hard to hit with props and orbs though

  • Capture the Flag: Work with your team to infiltrate the opposing teams’ base and take their flag back to yours. You receive 10 points for each capture

  • Fishing: Grab a rod, bait your hook, then bob it around in the water and nearly any asset from HL2 could spawn as well as a few secrets

  • Grav Only: You spawn with only a gravity gun, so go ham and kill everyone with anything you can throw

  • Jumps: Use strafe jumps and bhop to complete each map. A persistent checkpoint system is in place to allow you to save your progress

  • Killbox: Gravity is greatly reduced, fall damage has been disabled and you’ve been given a 25% speed boost

  • Surf: Slide down ramps to gain speed and use increased air control to help with transitions. The same checkpoint system as Jumps is enabled here

I'd like to thank everyone for your continued feedback and suggestions. Special thanks to Fay the Space Dog for helping come up with bot names!
