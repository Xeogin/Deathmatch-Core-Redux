What's the goal here?

Show off the best parts of HL2:DM in a way that keeps Pros happy while those with less experience can have fun too.

Full details on that in this Google Sheet.


What's changed with props?

They've been edited to make more sense mass-wise. Somethings are now too large or heavy to pick up.

Small props and the trap blade can be walked through when they're not moving, so there's less tripping and getting stuck.

Some props that were locked in-place previously can now be moved. E.g. the truck on Overwatch.

Explosive barrels and other destructable props now take 2 minutes to respawn. Twice the normal.



What's changed with weapons/health/armor?

Helped balanced Combine vs Rebel by multipling chest damage done to rebels by 2. Mr. Jingle finally has an upside.

The pistols damage has been reduced by 1, ar2 damage by 2, shotgun pellets by 3 and magnum by 30.

Double-barrel shotgun blasts have a basic lag compensation fix to improve reg.

Mag and RPG have a built-in zoom since not everyone knows about toggle_zoom. It's further than norm too, but it makes a noise.

SLAMs have been replaced by Combine Mines. Not very useful, but cute when trying to flip back over.

Orb ammo spawns as now armor batteries and rocket ammo spawns are orbs. This works surprisingly well on most maps.

Due to less orbs, max armor via Super Charger is reduced to only 150.



What's coming soon?

Spawning will fully invisible to make spawn killing/camping no longer an issue.

Kill, Specate and Jointeam are suppose to be time delayed to prevent trolling. For now they're blocked.

Removing deafening from explosions. They get in the way of conversations and tends to get stuck.

Reducing knock-back from being shot. Nades are fine, bullets shouldn't make you fly.

Triggers to play built-in sounds and voices. Adds character.
