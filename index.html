What's the goal here?

Show off the best parts of HL2:DM in a way that keeps Pros happy while helping those with less experience have fun too.
General changes are listed below and futher details are in this Google Sheet.


What's changed with props?
    

  • They've been edited to make more sense mass-wise. Somethings are now too large or heavy to pick up.
    • 

  • Small props and the trap blades can be walked through when they're not moving, so there's less tripping and getting stuck.
    • 

  • Some props that were locked in-place previously can now be moved. E.g. the truck on Overwatch.
    • 

  • Explosive barrels and other destructable props now take 2 minutes to respawn. Twice the normal.


What's changed with weapons/health/armor?
    

  • Helped balanced Combine vs Rebel by multipling chest damage done to rebels by 1.5. Mr. Jingle finally has some upside.
    • 

  • The pistol damage has been reduced by 1, ar2 and shotgun pellets by 2, and magnum by 30.
    • 

  • Many item spawns have been swapped around. It's both for balance and fun.
    • 

  • All charging is now twice as fast to keep things moving. The Super Charger maximum has been reduced to 150 to limit abuse.
    • 

  • Removed deafening from explosions, but that also reduced their total damage. Could be worse honestly.


What's coming soon?
    

  • Kill/Explode and Specate are suppose to be time delayed to prevent trolling and exploits. For now they're blocked.
    • 

  • Reducing knock-back from being shot. Nades are fine, bullets shouldn't make you fly.
